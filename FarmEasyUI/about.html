About Us

FarmEasy is a new vision to look into agricultural industries

Our vision is to provide the best services to those farmers who sells their crops in less price or with no margin and those crop buyers who buys crops through different vendors.

Using FarmEasy website, farmers can directly sell their products as per their convenience(quantity and price), and user can search for the best price and buy the crp directly through the farmers.

Steps to use FarmEasy website:-

As Farmer:-

  1. If new Farmer? Create profile as Farmer.
  2. Already have an account? Login with your credentials.
  3. In Farmer's homepage, select options of crops which you want to sell. Enter Quantity in KGs and Price.
  4. Submit button to sell the products
  5. Keep track your profile in Profile page.

As Buyer:-

  1. If new User? Create profile as Buyer.
  2. Already have an account? Login with your credentials.
  3. In Buyer's homepage, select options of crops which you want to buy. Enter Quantity in KGs and Price.
  4. Select the best fit farmer with your price and Contact with farmers.
  5. Keep track your profile in Profile page.
